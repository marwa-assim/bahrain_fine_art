Shaikh Isa Bin Salman Mosque

Overview:

A prominent mosque named after the late Amir of Bahrain, Shaikh Isa bin Salman Al Khalifa. It serves both religious and national significance and is known for its modern Islamic architecture.

Highlights:

  • Established in: Late 20th century
  • Main architectural feature: Large dome, tall minarets, and elegant calligraphy
  • Used for: Friday prayers, religious festivals, sermons

Location & Access:

Located in Riffa, near key roads and communities. Easily accessible with ample parking.
