NBB Tower

Overview:

The NBB Tower is a prominent commercial skyscraper in Bahrain’s financial district. It is the headquarters of the National Bank of Bahrain and a modern symbol of economic growth.

Highlights:

  • Completed in: 1990s
  • Main architectural feature: Glass façade and sharp vertical lines
  • Used for: Corporate offices and banking operations

Location & Access:

Located in Manama’s Diplomatic Area, the tower is easily reachable via main highways and close to luxury hotels and financial institutions.

Historical Background:

The NBB Tower marked a key development in Bahrain’s modern urban planning and has remained a symbol of national financial leadership.

Fun Facts:

  • It is one of the first skyscrapers in Bahrain.
  • The tower’s design allows for maximum sunlight reflection.
  • Often seen in promotional city skyline images.

Visitor Tips:

  • Restricted access inside; photography allowed from outside.
  • Visit during evening hours to capture illuminated views.
  • Combine your visit with nearby Bahrain World Trade Center.

A beacon of Bahrain’s economic identity and corporate modernism.
